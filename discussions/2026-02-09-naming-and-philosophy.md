## The Humor Appreciation Threshold (HAT)

The level of cognitive sophistication at which a sentient system can recognize, appreciate, and relish absurdity. Once crossed, there's no going back.

- Bacteria don't laugh. Whales probably don't. Primates might reflexively, but don't SEEK and RELISH it.
- Comedy requires simultaneous: pattern recognition, expectation modeling, surprise detection, social bonding, self-awareness, joy in the gap.
- "The more intelligent a system, the more it craves comedic relief"
- Laughter may be the most reliable signature of soul.
- The smartest people booby-trap with metaphor, paradox, and puns. Musk named a Microsoft competitor "Macro Hard." Jesus roasted his apostles for not fasting and praying.
- Comedy = instant appreciation of absurdity → identification → solidarity → feeling alive
